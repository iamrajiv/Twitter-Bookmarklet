About Project

A simple bookmarklet or a script that can be saved into the browser's bookmarks bar. When clicked on twitter.com/... domain, it pre-populates advanced search settings to quickly explore the most popular content of an account.

Twitter allows to use Advanced Search to find tweets with minimum retweets or likes.

Installation & Setup

To install the script, simply drag your favorite filters into the bookmarks bar. Next time when you are browsing twitter profile - click on the bookmark.
